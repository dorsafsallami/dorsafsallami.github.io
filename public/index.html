Dorsaf Sallami

Hello! This is Dorsaf! I’m a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the Université de Montréal , supervised by Professor Esma Aïmeur . My research interest is in the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Ethical AI, and Fake news. I am currently a part of the Artificial Intelligence for Cybersecurity Lab .

Before coming to Canada, I completed a five-year program, earning a Software Engineering degree in Tunisia. I was among the top four students nationwide, earning a scholarship in 2020 through a collaboration between the University of Montreal and the Tunisian Ministry of Higher Education, which allowed me to pursue a Master’s and Ph.D. program at the University of Montreal. I successfully obtained a Master’s in Computer Science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence at DIRO. Following that, I transitioned to a Ph.D. program in Artificial Intelligence in the fall of 2022, with my research centering on fake news and extending my Master’s thesis.
